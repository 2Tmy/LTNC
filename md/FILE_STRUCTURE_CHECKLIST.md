___ROLE
____
The Complainant (Client): External users who submit feedback and upload evidence (photos/documents) through the web portal.   
Customer Service Staff (Receptionist): Internal staff at Service Points or Coastal Radio Stations who receive the complaint, verify its validity, and log it into the digital "Tracking Book" (Form BM4).   
Specialized Specialist (Investigator): Experts assigned to find the root cause of the problem. The system ensures independence by not allowing the person involved in the mistake to investigate themselves.   
Management (Approver): Senior leaders who review the investigation results and officially authorize the final solution or compensation for the customer.       

ACTIVITY DIAGRAM AND WORKFLOW      Workflow Description:   

Initialize flow:   
The customer submits a complaint through the Web App system. The system records the complaint and basic information (customer details, complaint content, attached evidence).   
Receive and Validate complaint:   The Customer Service Department receives the complaint and checks its validity (Is the information complete? Is it within the company’s scope?).   If invalid, request additional information from the customer. If valid, proceed to the next step.   


Classify and Assign complaint:   The Customer Service Department classifies the complaint (product, service, staff behavior, etc.) and assigns it to the appropriate specialized department.   The system creates a “Pending complaint” record and transfers it to the processing side.   

Investigate complaint (Specialized Department):   The specialized department reviews the complaint, analyzes related information, and identifies the root cause.   Based on the investigation, a resolution plan is proposed.   Review and Approve (Management):   The resolution plan is submitted to management for review.   If the solution is not appropriate, it is returned for re-processing. If approved, proceed to response.   

Respond to customer:   The Customer Service Department communicates the resolution result to the customer via appropriate channels (system notification, email, phone, etc.).   Close / Reopen complaint:   If the customer accepts the result → the system closes the complaint and archives the record.   If the customer does not accept → the complaint is reopened or escalated for further handling.
